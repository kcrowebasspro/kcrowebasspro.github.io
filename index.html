Kevin Crowe

Kevin is an award-winning data journalist covering climate at The Washington Post. Before joining the Post, he worked for four years as an investigative data reporter at USA Today, where he covered a variety of subjects, including extreme weather, natural disasters, evictions and ambulance diversions. Before that, he was a data reporter for the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel, inewsource in San Diego and the St. Louis Post-Dispatch.
